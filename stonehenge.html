About Stonehenge

Stonehenge is one of the most famous prehistoric monuments in the world. Located in Wiltshire, England, it was built in several stages between around 3000 BC and 2000 BC. The monument is made up of massive standing stones arranged in circles and is believed to have been used for ceremonies, burials, or astronomical observations.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Wiltshire, England
  • Coordinates: 51.1789, -1.8262
  • Built: around 3000 BC to 2000 BC
  • Type: Prehistoric stone monument
  • Entrance Fee: Paid entry
  • Open to the public?: Yes

Note when going here

Bring a camera, wear comfortable clothes, and be ready for a bit of walking. The site can also be windy, so a light jacket is a good idea.
