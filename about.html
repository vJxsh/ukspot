This webpage was created as part of an academic project assigned by my instructor. The goal of this site is to showcase some of the most remarkable architectural tourism destinations in the United Kingdom. The UK is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and iconic structures that have influenced architecture and society around the world.

Many of these historic landmarks are open to the public, allowing visitors to explore centuries of history through castles, cathedrals, palaces, and other significant buildings. By visiting these places, people can better understand the purpose, design, and historical importance behind these structures while experiencing the atmosphere of the locations themselves.

This website aims to provide a simple guide to some of these architectural sites and encourage visitors to appreciate the history, culture, and craftsmanship that make the United Kingdom a fascinating destination for architectural tourism.
